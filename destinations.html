Exploring the Hidden Gem of Peshawar: Cadet College Warsak

Cadets Hostels♥

Cadet College Warsak is the first of its kind in Peshawar Division on the outskirts of Peshawar, the capital of KP. It is a project of the Frontier Corps Welfare Foundation under the Frontier Corps KP. The college covers an area of 60 Acres of land owned by the foundation at a picturesque site with scenic beauty and clean/serene ambience and pollution free environment in FC Garrison near the Army Air Defence Garrison. Located at the foothill of the mountainous ranges bifurcating the Khyber and Mohmand agencies by the Kabul River at the Warsak Dam. The master plan of the college envisages an ideal campus with lush green grounds, ornamental trees and gorgeous buildings to arouse the quest and craving of students to learn and grow with pride

Facilities offered in hostels

Accommodation:

Cadet College Warsak have four hostels/houses.
1.Iqbal House
2. Sher Shah Suri House
3. Shaheed House
4. Jinnah House
Cadets are provided with dormitory-style accommodation, usually with single beds with foam mattress, cupboards, and study tables.

Study Halls And TV Room:

Hostels typically have study halls where cadets are provided with best environment of study. The study halls are open 24/7 for cadets. They also had a TV Room for entertainment which is available only on sturday night till sunday.

House Master and House Tutor:

Cadet college hostels are supervised by House Master and House Tutor who ensure discipline, safety, and adherence to rules.

House Commanders:


Each house has four house commanders.First is house cammander,then senior section commander along with section commander and a sports sergeant. They groom cadets and ensure discipline in the house.

Study Time:

There are specific study times during which cadets are expected to focus on their academics.

Regulations:

Cadets are expected to follow a set of rules and regulations, including dress codes, quiet hours, and codes of conduct.

Cadets Mess♥

Cadet College Warsak's mess facilities play a pivotal role in providing nourishment and fostering a sense of community among the cadets. The mess at Cadet College Warsak is not just a place for meals; it's a hub of camaraderie and discipline. Cadets gather here to share their experiences, forge lasting friendships, and partake in wholesome, nutritious meals. The mess staff ensures that the cadets are well-fed, with a focus on balanced nutrition to support their physical and mental development. It is in these shared dining experiences that cadets strengthen their bonds and absorb the values of discipline and camaraderie that are integral to the cadet college experience at Warsak

Discipline in Mess

Discipline within the mess of a cadet college is of paramount importance. It is not solely about adhering to etiquettes and table manners but also an extension of the broader ethos of the institution. Cadets are expected to maintain a high standard of decorum during meal times, reflecting the core values of respect and orderliness. They learn the importance of punctuality, seating arrangements, and cleanliness, which are all essential attributes of military life. The mess serves as a microcosm of the disciplined life that cadets are being groomed for, and it instills in them the understanding that discipline is not limited to drills and parades but extends to all aspects of their daily routine, including their shared meals. This emphasis on discipline in the mess is a crucial element in the holistic education and character development that cadet colleges aim to provide.

Facilities in Cadets' Mess

The mess in a cadet college is a central facility designed to provide not only nutritious meals but also an environment that nurtures discipline, respect, and camaraderie. It includes a spacious dining hall with orderly seating arrangements and serving counters for meal distribution. Cadets are offered a variety of balanced menu options, and strict hygiene and cleanliness standards are upheld. A dedicated mess staff manages day-to-day operations, while cadet-run mess committees may oversee quality control. Discipline is paramount, with formal dress codes and supervised mealtimes. The mess serves as a training ground for cadets, teaching them not only dining etiquettes but also essential life skills and values, making it an integral component of the holistic cadet college experience.

MI Room♥

College has a well equipped 3 bedded MI Room under the supervision of Retired Medical JCO, assisted by 2 x Nursing NCOs. All minnor ailments are treated at the MI Room. For specialized treatment and in case of serious illness or injury the patient are referred to CMH Peshawar, Lady Reading Hospital or Hayatabad Medical Complex.

Library♥

A large portion in the first floor of senior academic block has been allocated to college library. The college library contains up to 1500 books in hard copies and up to 10,000 books in soft copies related to various subjects. The digital library has also got access to HEC digital library and up to 40,000 books on various subjects. Around 50 cadets can be accommodated at one time in college library where pin-drop silence is observed.
